About Us

Data For You And Me connects volunteer data scientists from around the world with nonprofits.

Nonprofits will post their challenges to a discussion board and data scientists will help to figure out what is needed to solve this challenge. Once the nonprofit views the challenge as capable to be solved, they will graduate the project to start the completion.
