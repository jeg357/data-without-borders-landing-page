About Data Without Borders

Data Without Borders is a collaboration tool provided by Domino Data Lab that connects volunteer data scientists from around the world with nonprofits. Our mission is to bring talent together to solve meaningful world problems. Nonprofits will post their challenges to a discussion board and data scientists will help to figure out what is needed to solve this challenge. Once the nonprofit views the challenge as capable to be solved, they will graduate the project to start the completion.
